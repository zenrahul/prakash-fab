Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you work with?

We specialize in metals such as steel, aluminum, and stainless steel.

Can you handle large volume orders?

Yes, we have the capacity to manage both small and large volume projects.

What is your turnaround time for a project?

Turnaround time varies based on the complexity and size of the project. Generally, we complete projects within 2-6 weeks.

Do you offer custom designs?

Absolutely! We work closely with clients to create custom designs tailored to their specifications.

What are your payment terms?

We typically require a 50% deposit upfront, with the remaining balance due upon completion of the project.

Can you assist with design and engineering?

Yes, we offer design and engineering assistance to ensure that your project meets all technical requirements and specifications.
